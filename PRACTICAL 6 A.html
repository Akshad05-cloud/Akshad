Tribute to Mahendra Singh Dhoni

The Legendary Captain of Indian Cricket

Key Achievements

  • Only captain to win all three ICC trophies (T20 World Cup 2007, ODI World Cup 2011, Champions Trophy 2013)
  • Led India to No. 1 position in ICC Test rankings for the first time
  • Most successful Indian captain with 178 wins across formats
  • Fastest stumping in cricket history (0.08 seconds)
  • First wicket-keeper to captain a team in 200+ ODIs
  • Padma Bhushan award (2018)
  • ICC ODI Player of the Year (2008, 2009)

About Dhoni

Mahendra Singh Dhoni, born July 7, 1981, is a former Indian cricketer and captain who is regarded as one of the greatest finishers in limited-overs cricket. Known for his calm demeanor and sharp cricketing mind, Dhoni revolutionized Indian cricket with his leadership and wicket-keeping skills.
